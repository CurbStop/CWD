About Us

  • We are an audit and consulting firm based in Maputo, Mozambique. Our presence in the Mozambican market can be traced back to 1984 when we operated primarily as an accountancy service provider.

    Since then our area of expertise has been refined and our primary focus is on providing audit, due diligence and general consulting for projects.

    We work in a competitive and growing market with our services directed to small and medium enterprises. As such our clients are those that seek a cost structure that is different to those typically found at much larger firms.

    We are present as an audit service provider in almost every industry. These include construction, general trade and NGOs.
